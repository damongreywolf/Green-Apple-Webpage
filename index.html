About the truck

Restaurant roots. Road-ready flavor.

Before it was a food truck, Green Apple was a sandwich shop in Ringgold, Georgia — a local spot built on fresh ingredients, warm toasted sandwiches, and flavor combinations people remembered. Now we’re taking those restaurant roots on the road, serving bold gourmet sandwiches across Northwest Georgia, Chattanooga, and surrounding areas with the same Green Apple twist.
